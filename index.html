THE PREMISE

Why Dhandha School needs to exist.

I. THE MBA IS A WALL.

The best business education in this country is gated. CAT scores, two-year programs, lakhs of rupees. For 99% of people who want to learn to start something, the exams and limited seats become a roadblock.

II. AI WILL TAKE YOUR JOB.

With the rise in capabilities of AI, most people will lose their jobs over time as their work gets automated. However, building something has never been easier. Its the best time to learn about business education and starting up.

III. CONTENT WITHOUT STRUCTURE.

The internet has every fact you need. What it doesn't have is order — a clear sequence, a curated voice, a syllabus designed by someone who has actually used what they're teaching.

IV. 63 MILLION DREAMERS.

India has 63 million MSMEs. Hundreds of millions more want to build something of their own. Almost none of them have access to real, structured, practical business education. That is the gap we exist to close.

Founders, aspiring entrepreneurs, college students, and working professionals who want to understand how businesses actually run financially. Built for people with zero finance background — no jargon, no prerequisites, just the stuff that matters when you're building something real.

None whatsoever. The entire masterclass is designed assuming you've never opened a balance sheet before. We start from first principles — what money actually means in a business — and build up from there. If anything feels unclear, the live Q&A is where you ask.
